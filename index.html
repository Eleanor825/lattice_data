The platform covers four model stages

One platform should own the data layer and the model-improvement layer together.

Pretrain

From scratch

Build corpora and structured records for new model training.

Continue

Adapt existing models

Continue training with scientific and domain-specific data.

Fine-tune

Optimize for tasks

Use compiled views for QA, instruction, and task adaptation.

Post-train

Align and safeguard

Support alignment, preference, and safety optimization in one story.

The product surface is low-friction

Users should feel like they are composing workflows, not wiring infrastructure by hand.

Chat

Describe the flow

Drag-drop

Reusable blocks

Reuse instead of rewiring

Turn complex flows into repeatable modules instead of one-off scripts.
